Generate a realistic, simulation-ready one-floor school environment based on a warm, open, welcoming school layout.

Overall concept:
Create a single-floor school with a central shared circulation space, 6 classrooms, 1 library, 2 separate restrooms (boys and girls), 1 storage room, and wide common circulation areas. The design should feel similar to a modern elementary or middle-school learning environment: warm wood materials, light-colored walls, indoor plants, natural lighting, organized classrooms, and clear circulation.

The style should match a clean, functional, inviting architectural school interior:
- warm neutral materials
- light wood furniture
- beige/light cream walls
- soft daylight
- visible windows in classrooms
- a pleasant and realistic school atmosphere
- not futuristic, not industrial, not luxury
- suitable for robot navigation and manipulation experiments

==================================================
OVERALL FLOOR LAYOUT
==================================================

Create one complete school floor with the following rooms and approximate arrangement:

- Classroom 1: lower-left side
- Classroom 2: lower-right side
- Classroom 3: middle-left side
- Classroom 4: upper-left side
- Classroom 5: upper-right side
- Classroom 6: middle-right side
- Library: centered near the lower middle, close to the main entrance
- Boys Toilet: left of the library / lower-left central zone
- Girls Toilet: adjacent to boys toilet in the same restroom zone
- Storage Room: middle-right area near Classroom 6 and Classroom 2
- Central circulation / common area: broad open interior space connecting all rooms
- Main entrance: bottom-center/front-facing side

The circulation should be intuitive and easy to navigate. The school should have a central spine / lobby-like common area with wide walkable paths and open visibility between spaces. The library should act as an important common academic space near the entrance. The classrooms should be arranged around the circulation zone.

Keep the environment consistent with a realistic single-floor school.
